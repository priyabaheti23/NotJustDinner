About Not Just Dinner

Not Just Dinner was created by Priya & Kiran to make slow dinners feel effortless and memorable. Kiran trained in Paris and blends refined technique with home-style warmth — every supper is a small, seasonal tasting menu designed to be shared. Priya curates the guest list and table ambience, ensuring an evening that feels like a warm living room conversation.
