About the Event

This isn’t just dinner — it’s one of those evenings people talk about after. A small, invite-only supper club in Bangalore, hosted by a Michelin-star-trained chef and her best friend.

What began as a shared dream has become an intimate table where strangers arrive curious and leave feeling oddly familiar.

Picture warm lighting, soft music, playful ice-breakers, and conversations that unfold as naturally as the courses themselves.

Every dish carries a story — of memories, ingredients, and moments — and for one night, you’re part of that narrative.

What to Expect

A thoughtfully crafted five-course, all-vegetarian menu (dessert contains egg), served in a space designed to feel less like a restaurant and more like a beautifully hosted dinner party.

Think cosy dinner-party energy — elevated food, effortless vibes, and people you haven’t met yet… but probably won’t forget.
